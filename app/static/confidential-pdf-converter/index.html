What counts as a confidential PDF?

A confidential PDF is any document where sharing the contents with the wrong person would cause a problem. That does not always mean top-secret material. It can be everyday business or personal paperwork that simply deserves careful handling.

Why not just use an online converter?

Online converters are excellent for ordinary files — public brochures, product sheets, school handouts and other low-risk documents. For those, the free online tool on pdf2image.online is fast and convenient.

Confidential files are different. With an online workflow, the PDF is selected in a browser and processed through an upload. With an offline workflow, the file never needs to leave your computer. That is a small change in habit, but it removes a lot of unnecessary worry.

If you would not email the PDF to a stranger, think twice before uploading it.
